Onboarding Assistant
I'm new here. What does 87% actually mean?
It means the market thinks there's an 87% chance this event happens. If you buy a YES share at 87 cents and it resolves YES, you get $1 back, so your profit is 13 cents per share. The lower the price, the higher your potential return, but the less likely the market thinks it'll happen.
What's a good market for a first trade?
Based on what's trending today, try the Fed rate decision (June) market. It's high-volume, well-understood, and resolves soon, so you'll see your result quickly. The current price is 87 cents YES.
