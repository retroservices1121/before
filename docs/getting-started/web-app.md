# Web App

The before web app at [b4enews.com](https://b4enews.com) is the fastest way to get AI intelligence briefs for any prediction market.

## How to use it

1. Visit [b4enews.com](https://b4enews.com)
2. Browse trending markets from Polymarket, Limitless, and Kalshi
3. Click any market to see the full detail page with an AI-generated context brief
4. The brief generates in real time, synthesizing market data, news, and historical context

## What's in a brief

Each brief includes:

- **Why this probability** - What's driving the current odds
- **Key factors** - Bullish, bearish, and neutral signals with sentiment labels
- **Historical base rate** - How similar events have resolved in the past
- **Upcoming catalysts** - Dates and events that could move the market

## Rate limits

During beta, all users get **2 free briefs per day**. Cached briefs (previously generated) don't count against your limit.

## Signing in

Click "Sign in" in the top-right corner to authenticate with your email. You'll receive a 6-digit code to verify your identity. Signing in lets you manage your account and access your API key for the Chrome extension.
