# Pricing

## Beta (current)

During beta, all users get **2 free briefs per day**. No account required.

Cached briefs (previously generated within the last 5 minutes) are served for free and don't count against your limit.

## Post-launch pricing

| Tier | Briefs/Day | Price |
|---|---|---|
| **Anonymous** | 2 | Free, no account needed |
| **Lite** | 5 | $4.99/mo |
| **Pro** | Unlimited | $9.99/mo |

## What counts as a brief

A brief is generated when the before API processes a market that isn't already cached. If another user recently requested the same market, you'll get the cached version for free.

## Managing your subscription

Sign in at [b4enews.com/account](https://b4enews.com/account) to:

- See your current plan and usage
- Upgrade from Lite to Pro
- Manage your subscription via Stripe
- Access your API key
