# before

**Know before it matters.**

before (B4E) is an AI-powered context engine for prediction markets. It auto-generates real-time intelligence briefs for markets on Polymarket, Limitless, Kalshi, and more, showing traders WHY a probability is where it is, what factors are driving it, and what to watch next.

## What before does

Every prediction market has a probability. Before tells you the story behind it.

For any market, before generates a structured intelligence brief that includes:

- **Why this probability** - A clear explanation of what's driving the current odds
- **Key factors** - The bullish, bearish, and neutral forces at play, with sentiment labels
- **Historical base rate** - How similar situations have played out in the past
- **Upcoming catalysts** - Events and dates that could move the market

## How it works

1. **Aggregate** - Real-time market data (odds, volume, price history) pulled from Polymarket, Limitless, and Kalshi via the Spredd API
2. **Crawl** - AI web agents scan relevant news, social signals, and data sources for each market
3. **Enrich** - Live token pricing, OHLCV, and risk data layered in for crypto markets
4. **Synthesize** - AI generates a structured, analyst-grade intelligence brief in seconds
5. **Deliver** - Briefs served through the web app, Chrome extension, and embeddable widget
